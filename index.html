Frequently Asked Questions

    When are tryouts?

There are no tryouts! As long as you're enthusiastic about tennis, you are welcome to come and practice!

    How much time do I have to commit to the team?

Club Tennis is all about putting in as much as you want to get out of it! We have two practices a week from 5:30-7 pm, as well as optional conditioning sessions every two weeks. Although practices are not mandatory for you to join the team, we do require at least 75% attendance for all members who want to play in tournaments.

    How much do I have to pay to play?

Dues are $20 per semester. This amount funds the team's indoor court-space, borrowed equipment, and other expenses like tournament fees and transportation.

    Who can I contact if I cannot make practice?

At the beginning of every week, you will receive an email from our amazing Logistics chair regarding practice signups. If you aren't free that week, just don't put your name down! In addition, if you do sign up and CANNOT make the practice, you are free to remove your name or tell someone on exec, as long as this happens 12 hours before practice. If you do sign up for practice and do not show, you will get a warning. Once you receive 3 warnings, you will be kicked off of the team.

    I haven't played in years. How good is everyone?

We have players with all different experience levels! Practices are designed so that you'll have a chance to play people at both your skill level, as well as other skill levels! If you don't want to play people that are more experienced, just let the exec member running practice know, and they will try to pair you up with someone in the same boat! Regardless, there's no need to worry about not being good - we're all in this to get better!



If you have any further questions, feel free to contact any member on exec!
