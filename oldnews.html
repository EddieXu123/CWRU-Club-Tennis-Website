Feburary 2020

The University announced that classes will become remote for the rest of the school year. In addition, meetings of more than 25 individuals will not be allowed. Unfortunately, this restriction includes club tennis. We've had a great year and it's been super fun watching everyone improve together. To all the seniors graduating, we wish you luck in your future endeavors. You'll always be welcome to come back and hit around! To the rest of you, have a great rest of the semester and we hope to see you back on the courts in the Fall!

October 2019

The team traveled down to Dayton, Ohio this weekend for the annual Dayton vs. CWRU tournament. The format of the tournament was 4 singles and 2 doubles matches. Freshmen Michael, Amy, and Selena played extremely hard-fought sets, edging over their opponents. Junior and VP Tim clutched a win against his opponent, taking home the trophy for CWRU. To quote Alan Dogan's Instagram post, we "served up so many bagels this weekend Dayton ran out of creem cheese""
